History & Resources

Chyke Collins International Limited was Established in 2001 as a private limited company which has its head quarter in Abuja, Nigeria.

In 2010, we established Desire Finishing Limited to compliment the activities of our parent company. And together we are known as Chyke Collins Group.

As an aspirational group, Chyke Collins Group of Companies has established itself as a pacesetter in a fast based business as a manufacturer of building and construction materials with business relationships in China and Turkey. Contributing to globalization through foreign trades in quality building materials for the high and middle-class market. 

In Nigeria, our Bastion in the trading world has earned us the trust of thousands of people in the construction industry.
